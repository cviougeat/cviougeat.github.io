IDefender

Our project addresses the need for anonymity in blockchain transactions, a feature often compromised in this technology. Leveraging the sapling protocol, we've created a solution enabling companies to conduct transactions anonymously. Unique to our approach is the ability to deanonymize transactions at the request of governance bodies, maintaining accountability.
